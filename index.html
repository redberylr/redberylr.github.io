Safara Water Limited, incorporated on April 23, 2013, in Kontagora, Niger State, Nigeria, is a premier provider of spring water. Registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission under registration number 1110130, our office is located at Km 2 Road, along F.C.E Road, Kontagora.

Our flagship product, branded as Goje Water, carries NAFDAC registration number C1-5926 and the Mandatory Conformity Assessment Program (MANCAP) certification, underscoring our commitment to quality and safety.
